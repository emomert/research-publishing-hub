# Did COVID-19 Hollow Out the Middle Class? Wage and Consumption Inequality Within the OECD Middle Class After the Pandemic

## What this article covers
OECD middle-class inequality after COVID-19, with emphasis on wage and consumption inequality within the middle class.

## Hypothesis question
Did the COVID-19 period increase inequality within the middle class in OECD countries by pushing previously middle-class households toward lower- and higher-income segments, with especially visible increases in wage and consumption inequality?

## Research topics
- Post-COVID wage distribution dynamics across OECD countries
- Real wage erosion and recovery by wage decile
- Within-middle consumption inequality during/after pandemic
- Whether middle-income groups were compressed downward vs polarized
- Interaction between pandemic policy support, inflation, and distributional outcomes
